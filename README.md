# Accounting, From First Principles

An interactive web textbook introducing accounting from the ground up — built for teens with no prior background.

🌐 **Live site:** https://ujin-kkk.github.io/accounting-textbook-web/

## About

A self-paced digital textbook designed so students with zero accounting background can follow from page one. Eight Parts, forty chapters, with worked examples, instant-feedback quizzes, glossaries, and case studies on real US public companies (Apple, Microsoft, Tesla, Delta, Marriott, and more).

## Curriculum

1. **Why Accounting?** — Why it exists and how it works as the language of business
2. **Recording Transactions** — The accounting equation, debits and credits, journals, ledgers
3. **Financial Statements** — Income statement, balance sheet, cash flow, changes in equity
4. **Accounting Through Real Companies** — From coffee shops to airlines and platforms
5. **Financial Analysis and Investing** — Ratios, liquidity, solvency, earnings manipulation
6. **Introduction to Auditing** — Audit roles, internal controls, audit opinions
7. **New and Emerging Fields** — Space, AI, ESG, platform-economy accounting
8. **Projects and Practical Exercises** — Hands-on capstone projects

## Features

- 📖 Automatic per-chapter progress saving (browser local storage)
- ✅ Multiple-choice quizzes with instant feedback + free-response essay prompts
- 🎨 Playful academic design — color blocks, geometric icons, editorial typography
- 🏢 All examples drawn from US public companies (verifiable via SEC EDGAR)
- 📱 Fully responsive — works on desktop, tablet, and phone

Click the Live site link above. No installation or sign-up required.
